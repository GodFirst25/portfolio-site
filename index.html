About Me

I'm a passionate creative technologist at the intersection of film, music, and technology. With a background in computer science and a love for storytelling, I blend cinematic visuals, dynamic audio, and code to build immersive experiences that resonate.

Whether I'm designing intuitive user interfaces, editinng videos, producing music-driven visuals, or experimenting with interactive media, my work lives at the crossroads of creativity and code. I'm especially interested in how sound and visulas can work together-like syncing motion to music or building interactive tools that respond to rhythm and tone.

I believe technology isn't just functional, it's expressive. I use it as a medium to craft moments that feel cinematic, sound immersive, and work seamlessly.
